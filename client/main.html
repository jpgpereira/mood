What if HOOD wasn’t just an office in black and wood,
but a living space colored by everyone’s emotions?
Wouldn’t it be easier to empathize if you could see how others are feeling? Yes, it would.

MOOD is a platform that tracks the emotional state of HOOD’s inhabitants, and transforms that data into visual outputs to promote empathy. Pick up the tablet and choose the emotion that most resembles your current mood (the “Wheel of Emotions” might be helpful). The lamp at the bar will light up with your mood, and then change back to the overall HOOD mood.
